✦ Through the Ages ✦

Every Era, Every Story

A journey through the evolution of Japanese animation

Origins
1917 – 1950s
創世記 — Sōseiki
The birth of Japanese animation. Hand-drawn silhouettes and short theatrical films emerged, inspired by Western pioneers. Studios like Toei Animation were founded, laying the foundation for an entire art form.
1917Namakura Gatana
1918Urashima Tarō
1945Momotarō: Sacred Sailors
Golden Age
1960s – 1970s
黄金時代 — Ōgon Jidai
Osamu Tezuka revolutionised anime with manga-influenced character design. TV anime exploded in popularity with mecha, sentai, and science fiction themes capturing the imagination of a generation.
1963Astro Boy
1966Jungle Emperor Leo
1972Devilman
1974Heidi of the Alps
1979Mobile Suit Gundam
Renaissance
1980s
復興 — Fukkō
The OVA market boomed and theatrical films achieved artistic heights never seen before. Studio Ghibli was born. Dark, mature storytelling pushed anime beyond children's entertainment into a global art form.
1982Macross
1983Urusei Yatsura 2
1984Nausicaä
1986Castle in the Sky
1988Akira
1988Grave of the Fireflies
1988My Neighbor Totoro
Global Awakening
1990s
世界への目覚め — Sekai e no Mezame
Anime reached Western shores in earnest. Philosophical mecha, magical girls, and cyberpunk thrillers redefined what animation could say. Anno, Oshii, and Miyazaki produced their masterpieces.
1992Sailor Moon
1993Patlabor 2
1995Ghost in the Shell
1995Neon Genesis Evangelion
1997Princess Mononoke
1998Cowboy Bebop
1999One Piece
Digital Era
2000s
デジタル時代 — Dejitaru Jidai
Digital ink replaced cels entirely. Anime found a global fanbase through internet streaming. Spirited Away won the Academy Award. Isekai, shōnen battle epics, and slice-of-life genres flourished.
2001Spirited Away
2003Fullmetal Alchemist
2003Wolf's Rain
2006Paprika
2007Gurren Lagann
2009Eden of the East
Streaming Age
2010s
配信時代 — Haishin Jidai
Netflix, Crunchyroll, and Funimation transformed distribution. Record-breaking films, landmark series, and a new generation of studios defined anime as the world's most-watched animation style.
2011Madoka Magica
2013Attack on Titan
2016Your Name
2017Made in Abyss
2019Weathering with You
2019Demon Slayer
Now & Beyond
2020s – Present
現代 — Gendai
Anime dominates global pop culture. Record cinema runs, AI-assisted production, and a new wave of creators push boundaries. The imagination has no limits — the story never ends.
2021Jujutsu Kaisen 0
2021Evangelion: 3.0+1.0
2022The Boy and the Heron
2023Suzume
2024Dungeon Meshi
2025+The Future Awaits…
✦ About This Realm ✦

Live in Your Imagination

This realm is a love letter to Japanese animation — to every hand-drawn frame, every impossible world, every character who taught us what it means to be brave, gentle, or endlessly curious. From flickering black-and-white silhouettes of 1917 to the luminous theatrical spectacles of today, anime is not just entertainment. It is a portal. A language. A way of dreaming.
